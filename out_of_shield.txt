Please note that aerials have 3 frames added to their startup to account for the jump squat duration. Grabs require 4 frames of additional startup as we are accounting for a scenario where an attack is blocked. Finally, any attack that isn't an Up Smash, Up Special, grab, aerial, or item toss has had an additional 11 frames of startup applied to it.
